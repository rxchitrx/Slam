# Manual Device Checklist (LiDAR iPhone)

- Confirm app launches and Home screen appears.
- Tap `Start Room Scan`, grant camera permission if prompted.
- Scan a small room for 60-90 seconds.
- Verify live debug values update (tracking, elapsed, coverage, confidence).
- Verify completion suggestion banner appears after sufficient coverage/stability.
- Tap `Stop` and confirm scan is saved.
- Return to Home and verify `Open Latest Scan` is enabled.
- Open latest scan and verify in-app 3D orbit interaction works.
- Open Quick Look from preview and confirm model loads.
- Tap `Export USDZ` and verify share sheet appears.
- Relaunch app and confirm latest scan remains available.
- Deny camera permission in Settings and verify the scan flow reports a clear error.
